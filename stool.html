Design & fabrication of rectilinear furniture.

[Description]

Design a stool using only 4 boards, joined through 90 degree relations.
[Tools & Processes]

Designed with scale models SolidWorks. Fabricated using poplar wood. Tools used include joiner & planer, chopsaw, tablesaw, bandsaw, jigsaw, router, chisel, sanders.
[Collaborators & Duration]

Collaborated with Gioia Pirozzi, Felix Cooper, over the course of 3 weeks.
[Introduction]

Designer & Fabricator


The stool I designed was fabriacted by Gioia Pirozzi, and I helped fabricate a stool designed by Felix Cooper. This project was an exercise in communication between designer and 'manufacturer', where our primary mode of communication was through construction drawings.
^Stool designed by me, fabricated by Gioia Pirozzi.
